Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

BioGrowth Merchants LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the regulatory privacy and data integrity of our corporate clients, platform merchants, and big-box enterprise retail partners. This Privacy Policy details the exact nature of business data handled through our digital portal.

1. Enterprise Information Collection

We restrict data gathering exclusively to professional information willingly processed through our secure RFP/RFQ Inquiry System. This data includes: Legal Company Name, Procurement Officer Name, Corporate Email Identification, and specific logistics/SKU volume metrics requirements.

2. Deployment of Collected Data

The processing of submitted parameters is isolated solely to facilitating corporate vendor qualifications analysis, structuring large-scale price quotes, and verifying phytosanitary or logistics alignment. No business intelligence data is distributed, rented, or leased to independent third-party operators.

3. Institutional Data Security

We deploy standard cryptographic safeguards and strict internal access limitations to shield your trade requirements from loss, disclosure, interception, or unauthorized modifications.
