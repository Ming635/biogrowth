USDA & Federal Seed Act Compliance Statement

Last Updated: July 2026

BioGrowth Merchants LLC executes all agricultural operations under uncompromising adherence to commercial crop safety, ecological protection, and rigorous administrative compliance frameworks throughout North America.

1. Federal Seed Act Compliance

Every flower and botanical seed lot cataloged under our manufacturing grid is processed, isolated, and accurately documented in total harmony with the rules established by the United States Federal Seed Act (FSA). We maintain extensive records regarding purity standards, rigorous mechanical cleaning, and the total exclusion of regional noxious weed elements.

2. USDA Logistics & Phytosanitary Control

Our logistical tracking operates in strict coordination with standard United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) regulatory demands. All distributed volume units assigned to commercial or store shelves are accompanied by verifiable phytosanitary certifications to protect ecological safety and ensure cross-border tracking accountability.

3. Multi-Stage Germination Protocols

Continuous internal and third-party laboratory verification processes are active to ensure that standard batch germination performance consistently exceeds federal and state minimum legal benchmarks before the product lines undergo retail-ready shelf retail branding and packaging deployment.
